Why SCCM?

Cultivating Excellence

Soundscapes Conservatory of Music has become a beacon of music education in Brooklyn since its establishment in 1981. Committed to excellence, SCCM offers high quality musical education with a blend of traditional and modern approaches for students of all levels and ages.

Our Core Values

Innovation

At SCCM, we embrace each student's distinct journey in music. Our tailored approach designs a unique curriculum that complements individual aspirations and talents.

Mastery

Our curriculum emphasizes rigorous preparation for formal music examinations and professional pursuits, setting a solid foundation for future success.

Expression

Our semi-annual events showcase our students' progress and artistry, from recitals to exhibitions, nurturing a community that celebrates achievement and confidence.

Expertise

Our faculty are seasoned professionals with advanced degrees and a passion for imparting wisdom, providing an enriching education to learners at all stages.
